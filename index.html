About

A Strategic Learning Partner for Your Workforce

I am a Founder | Workforce Transformation Consultant with 15 plus years of experience designing and delivering high-impact learning programmes for corporate employees, first-time managers, campus graduates and leadership teams. I specialise in helping people communicate with clarity, carry themselves with presence and perform with confidence, translating behavioural science into practical and memorable learning.

My approach combines experiential facilitation, real-world application and personalised coaching, designed to create lasting behavioural change and not just one-day awareness.

I also bring an AI-empowered approach to facilitation and content design, helping professionals and teams build practical fluency with tools like Claude, ChatGPT and Gemini so they can think, write and communicate faster without losing their own voice.

Communication & Presentation Professional Presence & Image Emotional Intelligence (EQ) Leadership Presence Growth Mindset Facilitation AI Literacy & Prompt Engineering Interview & Career Readiness Train the Trainer (TTT)

My Learning Philosophy

I believe learning creates lasting behavioural change only when people experience, practise, receive feedback and commit to action. Every programme I design blends reflection, simulation, coaching and workplace application, so participants leave with new habits, not just new knowledge.
